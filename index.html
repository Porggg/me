About

Second year student at Grenoble INP - Ensimag.

Grenoble INP - Ensimag is a top engineering school in France, specializing in computer science and applied mathematics. Founded in 1960, it is part of the University Grenoble Alpes (UGA) and recognized internationally. Located in a dynamic ecosystem of research labs and industries, Ensimag trains engineers with a dual expertise in mathematics and computer science, preparing them for careers in diverse sectors such as energy, finance, and technology.

The MMIS program (Modeling, Mathematics, and Computer Science for Data Science) focuses on training engineers in numerical modeling, multimedia, scientific computing, and statistical analysis. Graduates pursue careers as consulting or research engineers, applying their skills to complex systems across various industries.
